Pertanika Journals

Deliver the highest quality research to the widest possible audience

Pertanika journals are international peer-reviewed journals devoted to the publication of original papers. They serve as a forum for practical approaches to improving quality in issues pertaining to tropical agriculture and its related fields, science, and engineering research, and also deals in research or theories in social and behavioural sciences as well as the humanities, particularly within the Asia Pacific region.
