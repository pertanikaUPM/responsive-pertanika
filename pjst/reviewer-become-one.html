How To Be A Reviewer of Pertanika?

To be a reviewer of Pertanika, simply download the Reviewer's Application Form, complete it, and send it to the Chief Executive Editor via email. You will be informed of the outcome of your application within 2 weeks of your submission.

  • Download Reviewer's Application Form

Please submit the 'Reviewer's Application Form' and your CV to the Chief Executive Editor via email to executive_editor.pertanika@upm.edu.my.

Reviewer should read the “Manuscript Reviewing Guide” below before evaluating the manuscripts using the ScholarOneTM Online Submission system.
