ONLINE SUBMISSION

Welcome! The Pertanika Journal of Science & Technology accepts submissions through its Online Submission system which is supported by Clarivate Analytics.

Owing to the volume of manuscripts we receive, we must insist that all submissions be made via our online submission system.

Using this system, authors can upload manuscript files (text, figures, and supplementary information) directly to our office and check on the status of their manuscripts via e-alerts during the review process. In addition, reviewers can access the manuscript (in a highly secure fashion that maintains referee anonymity) over a direct internet link, which speeds the review process. Revisions should be uploaded via the link provided in the editor's decision letter. Please do not submit revisions as new manuscripts.

Log In

Go to ScholarOneTM submission site: http://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/upm-jst to bring up the Log In screen.

Return to "Log-in" and click on either "Create an Account" (if you are a first-time user), or "Check for Existing Account" (if you have previously created an account).

Procedure

At the ScholarOne submission site, under "Resources", Click on "Instructions and Forms" to view the "Guide to Online Submissions" to familiarise yourself with the procedure. For the preparation of the manuscript, you may view the "Instructions to Authors".

Submission

Log in, click on "Author Center", then on "Submit a New Manuscript", and follow the prompts and instructions given on the screen until your manuscript (text, figures, tables) has been entered.

Submit Now!

If you experience any problems using the system, please click here Get Help Now or contact the Journal's Editorial Office via email to journal.officer-2@upm.edu.my
