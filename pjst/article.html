Former and Current Trend in Subsurface Irrigation Systems

Natarajan Sundaram, Cherian Thomas and Loganathan Agilandeeswari

Pertanika Journal of Social Science and Humanities, Volume 29, Issue 1, January 2021

DOI: https://doi.org/10.47836/pjst.29.1.01

Date Published: 14 Dec. 2020

Abstract

The main purpose of this study was to investigate the relationship between tourist experience, tourist motivation and destination loyalty for historic and cultural tourists. A survey of 1,389 tourists who visited Sheqi, an ancient town in center China was conducted as the basis for empirical analysis. The results of this study revealed that touristsâ€™ experience influenced destination loyalty positively, and tourist motivation mediated the effect of tourist experience on destination loyalty significantly.
